Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?
You can update your details by navigating to Profile → Edit Profile. Make sure to save your changes.
Why is my plate not recognized?
The system might not detect plates if they are blurry, too bright, or blocked. Try cleaning the plate and parking within the scan zone.
How do I report inaccurate detections?
Use the “Submit a Report” button above, and attach details such as time, plate number, and issue description.
